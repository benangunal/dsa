How to submit

📋 Submission guide

1
Open in Google Colab

Click an "Open in Colab" button above. Sign in with your university Google account — no download needed.

2
Run the timer cell first

The first cell starts your session tracker. Then fill in STUDENT_ID, STUDENT_NAME, STUDENT_EMAIL, and CLASS_CODE.

3
Complete the exercises

Each notebook has 4 exercises marked ✏️ [EX1] – [EX4]. Run each cell after writing your solution. A stretch goal is included for fast finishers.

4
Run the Submit cell

The final cell collects your code and sends it automatically. You will receive an email confirmation. Resubmission is allowed — only the last one counts.

5
AI is allowed

Claude, Copilot, ChatGPT — all permitted. You must understand every line. Copy-paste without understanding = zero during the oral check.
